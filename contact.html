Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule a campus tour by contacting our admissions office at +91 22 1234 5680 or by filling out the campus tour request form on our website. Tours are available on weekdays from 10 AM to 3 PM.

Most departments are open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. The admissions office has extended hours during peak application periods. Specific department hours may vary, so it's best to call ahead.
