Our Faculty

Meet our distinguished faculty members who are leading experts in pharmaceutical sciences, research, and education.

Distinguished Faculty Members

Our faculty comprises experienced educators, researchers, and industry professionals dedicated to advancing pharmaceutical education and research.

Dr. Anjali Mehta

Professor & Head, Pharmaceutics

Drug Delivery Systems Nanotechnology Formulation Development

Dr. Mehta specializes in novel drug delivery systems with over 15 years of research experience. She has published more than 80 research papers in international journals.

Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Professor, Pharmacology

Neuropharmacology Drug Discovery Preclinical Research

Dr. Kumar's research focuses on neurodegenerative diseases and novel therapeutic approaches. He has received multiple national awards for his contributions to pharmacology.

Dr. Priya Sharma

Associate Professor, Medicinal Chemistry

Drug Design Molecular Modeling Synthetic Chemistry

Dr. Sharma's research involves computer-aided drug design and synthesis of novel therapeutic agents. She has several patents to her name in anticancer drug development.

Dr. Sanjay Patel

Professor, Pharmacy Practice

Clinical Pharmacy Pharmacotherapy Patient Safety

Dr. Patel is a clinical pharmacist with extensive hospital experience. His research focuses on medication therapy management and improving patient outcomes.

Dr. Meera Joshi

Professor, Pharmacognosy

Natural Products Herbal Medicine Phytochemistry

Dr. Joshi specializes in the study of medicinal plants and natural product drug discovery. She has documented numerous traditional medicinal plants with therapeutic potential.

Dr. Vikram Singh

Associate Professor, Pharmaceutics

Biopharmaceutics Pharmacokinetics Drug Absorption

Dr. Singh's research focuses on drug absorption mechanisms and formulation strategies to enhance bioavailability. He has collaborated with several pharmaceutical companies.
