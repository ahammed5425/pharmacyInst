Admission Process

1

Application

Submit your application online along with required documents and application fee.

2

Entrance Test

Appear for the PharmaCare Entrance Test (PCET) or submit valid scores of national tests.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview with the admission committee.

4

Admission Offer

Selected candidates will receive admission offers and need to complete the enrollment process.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid score in PCET or other national level entrance tests
  • Proof of age and identity
  • Transfer certificate from previous institution
  • Medical fitness certificate

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy or related field
  • Minimum 55% marks in qualifying degree
  • Valid score in GPAT or PCET-PG
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • Research proposal (for Ph.D programs)
