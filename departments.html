Academic Departments

Our departments are at the forefront of pharmaceutical education and research, each specializing in key areas of pharmaceutical sciences.

Department of Pharmaceutics

Focuses on drug formulation, delivery systems, and pharmaceutical technology. Research areas include nanotechnology, controlled release systems, and novel drug delivery approaches.

Research Formulation
Explore

Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Dedicated to drug design, synthesis, and analysis. Research focuses on medicinal chemistry, computational drug design, and analytical method development.

Synthesis Analysis
Explore

Department of Pharmacology

Studies drug actions, mechanisms, and therapeutic uses. Research areas include neuropharmacology, cardiovascular pharmacology, and toxicology.

Mechanisms Toxicology

Department of Pharmacognosy

Focuses on medicinal plants, natural products, and herbal medicine. Research includes phytochemistry, standardization of herbal products, and ethnopharmacology.

Natural Products Herbal Medicine
Explore

Department of Pharmacy Practice

Focuses on clinical pharmacy, patient care, and healthcare outcomes. Emphasizes evidence-based practice, pharmacotherapy, and patient counseling.

Clinical Patient Care
Explore

Department of Pharmaceutical Biotechnology

Integrates biotechnology with pharmaceutical sciences. Research areas include recombinant DNA technology, monoclonal antibodies, and gene therapy.

Research Focus Areas

Neuropharmacology

Developing novel therapeutics for neurological disorders including Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, and epilepsy.

Infectious Diseases

Research on antimicrobial resistance, novel antibiotics, and antiviral therapies.

Personalized Medicine

Pharmacogenomics and tailored therapies based on individual genetic profiles.

Drug Delivery Systems

Advanced delivery technologies including nanoparticles, liposomes, and transdermal systems.

Natural Products

Discovery and development of therapeutics from medicinal plants and marine organisms.

Computational Pharmacy

In silico drug design, molecular modeling, and AI-assisted drug discovery.
