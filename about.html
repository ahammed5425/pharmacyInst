About PharmaCare University

Discover our rich history, mission, and vision as we continue to shape the future of pharmaceutical education and research.

Our Story

Established in 1985, PharmaCare University has been at the forefront of pharmaceutical education and research in India. Our institution was founded with the vision to create a center of excellence that would produce skilled professionals capable of meeting the evolving healthcare needs of society.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small college to a comprehensive university with multiple campuses, state-of-the-art facilities, and a diverse student body from across the globe. Our commitment to innovation and excellence has positioned us as a leader in pharmaceutical sciences education.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional pharmaceutical education through innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and meaningful community engagement. We strive to develop competent, ethical, and compassionate healthcare professionals who can address global health challenges.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in pharmaceutical education, research, and healthcare innovation. We envision a future where our graduates lead transformative changes in pharmaceutical sciences and improve health outcomes worldwide.

Our Journey

1985

PharmaCare University was established with a vision to revolutionize pharmaceutical education in India.

1995

Introduced postgraduate programs and established our first research center for drug discovery.

2005

Expanded to include Pharm.D program and established international collaborations with universities in the US and Europe.

2015

Launched our state-of-the-art research facility and achieved NAAC A+ accreditation.

2020

Played a pivotal role in COVID-19 research and established the Center for Pharmaceutical Innovation.

2023

Expanded our campus with new facilities and launched online learning platforms to reach global students.
